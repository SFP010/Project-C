We are a Platform where talented artist from around the globe can easily merchandise their artworks without having to do all the hard and boring work of marketing, productions and transportations.

Company history

We started as a school project in the fall of 2019 by four classmates from Hogeschool in Rotterdam. We realized that there is a real need for this type of service after working on this project for a few months. After the school project was finished, we had decided to make this into a real company and the rest is history.
