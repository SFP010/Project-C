1. How to add a product with a design for my artwork? (Only for an artist account holder)

Step 1

Login with an artist account. You can check your account status on your profile page.

Step 2

Upload an artwork by clicking on the upload artwork button on your profile page. (You can skip this step if you have already uploaded an artwork with your account)

Step 3

Click on My artworks button on your profile page. On that page you can see all the artworks that you have uploaded so far.

Step 4

Click on the button called “Add/Edit Product” of the artwork that you want to make the design for.

Step 5

Choose the product that you want to add the design to and click “Add product design” button.

Step 6

You can change the position of the artwork by dragging it by the frame. The frame will appear when you hover over the art work with your mouse cursor. You can set the width and height of the artwork-frame with the control panel on the right side of the screen. Do not forget to save your design after you have finished designing.
